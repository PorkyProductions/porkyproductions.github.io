The PorkyProductions Origin Story

Our story begins in 2017, when our founders, Tristan and Ryan, met in school. They became friends and eventually discovered that they could make a website using Google Sites. What better thing to make a website about than a hedgehog pencil sharpener Tristan had named "Porky".

After PorkyPictures.inc was created, many other websites followed: PorkySchool.edu, PorkyPizza.eat, and PBHTWDTEUSYPSUT.c.c.i. Porky had developed a cult following.

Unfortunately, a disgusting hater stole the pencil sharpener and destroyed it. During his last moments, Porky transferred into a hedgehog plush, where he resides to this day.

The old websites were archived and consolidated into one company — PorkyProductions, the site you're visiting now.
